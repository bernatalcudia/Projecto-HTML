PROFESSIONAL VIDEO GAME LEAGUE

LVP, part of the MEDIAPRO Group, is one of the largest operators of video game competitions in the world and the largest esports organisation in the Spanish-speaking world.

Through its offices in Madrid, Barcelona, Buenos Aires, Bogota and Mexico, it is present in more than 30 countries. LVP organises the most prestigious national competitions and major international tournaments, has online competition platforms (ArenaGG) and distributes worldwide events such as the League of Legends European Championship (c) in Spanish. LVP also provides event production and audiovisual production services.

OUR HISTORY

LVP is a specialist in the organisation of national competitions. It has almost a decade of experience in Spain, where it currently organises the League of Legends Superliga and Iberian Cup, the Unity League and the VALORANT Rising Series.

For more than two years now, as part of its international expansion strategy, LVP has made the leap to Latin America, where it operates the Master Flow League in Argentina, the Entel Honor League in Chile, the Honor Division in Mexico, the Golden League in Colombia, the Claro Gaming Stars League in Peru and the Volcano League Presented by TVCable in Ecuador.

In this sense, LVP stands out for structuring the national competitive ecosystem from its base, consolidating the teams, reinforcing their identities, connecting with the local public and generating stories around the competition and its protagonists.
