VALORANT'S RISING MEDIAMARKT AND INTEL DEFINE THE SIX TEAMS THAT WILL FIGHT FOR THE TITLE AND PROMOTION TO EUROPE

Giants, KOI, CASE Esports, Rebels Gaming, Team Queso and UCAM Tokiers qualified for the playoffs yesterday in an exciting ninth matchday where almost everything was still to be decided before the start. In fact, only three teams (Arctic, BISONS ECLUB and Heretics) started their match without playoff qualification options. Between the first and the seventh, however, the six places for the battle for the title were to be shared. Although most of them had already secured their tickets, it remained to be decided in which position Rising MediaMarkt and Intel would finish, which could be decisive for their future in the playoffs.

OMEN, NEW SPONSOR OF THE NATIONAL VALORANT COMPETITIONS ORGANISED BY LVP

OMEN will be featured in VRL Spain: Rising MediaMarkt and Intel, the new Spanish league of VALORANT, where it will have its own section: the "clutch moments", that is, all those plays in which the last player of a team eliminates two or more of its enemies to end up winning the round against the odds. OMEN will also be present in other moments of the broadcast, as well as on LVP's social networks.

LEGACY CUP KICKS OFF, AN AMATEUR FIFA 22 COMPETITION FOR LATIN AMERICA, MANAGED BY LVP

The FIFA 22 Legacy Cup, the amateur competition that takes place in several Latin American countries and offers $10,000 in prizes, as well as next-generation consoles, is kicking off. LEAGUE OF LEGENDS MASTER FLOW LEAGUE RETURNS ON JANUARY 26TH TO LVP ARGENTINA

The Master Flow League is back, the official League of Legends competition in Argentina organised by LVP together with Flow. On January 26th, from 18:00 hours, the new season of the tournament begins with important new features compared to the previous edition. Kru Esports, Sergio Kun Agüero's team, makes its debut in the Argentine league with a team made up of great representatives of the scene led by Martín "Coscu" Pérez Disalvo. On the other hand, there will be two big returns: 9z, formed by one of the largest communities in the region, and Malvinas Gaming, one of the most historic teams of the local scene.
